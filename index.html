About

East Fifth Capital acquires and manages commercial real estate with a focus on long-term value creation. We target well-located industrial and multifamily properties in growth markets across the Southeast.

Our approach combines institutional discipline with hands-on asset management, emphasizing stable cash flow, operational efficiency, and capital preservation.

Investment Focus

Industrial

Light industrial, flex, and warehouse properties serving established tenants in growing metro areas.

Multifamily

Workforce and market-rate apartment communities in supply-constrained Southeast markets.

Value-Add

Properties with below-market rents, deferred maintenance, or operational upside where active management drives returns.
